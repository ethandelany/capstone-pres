SUU YEA Photo App

By Ethan Delany, Ethan Fletcher, Yubin Hu

Project Overview

This is a project designed to help the SUU Youth Engineering Academy (YEA) manage photo distribution to parents and for marketing.

It allows staff members to take and upload photos, allows the camp supervisor to approve & automatically send these photos to the parents of the campers.
